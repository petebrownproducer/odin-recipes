Pasta Pomodoro

Description

A classic, light Italian pasta dish made with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and basil.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or Penne
  • Canned San Marzano tomatoes (or fresh cherry tomatoes)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Parmesano Reggiano
  • Salt and red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water.
  2. Add plenty of salt to the water.
  3. Saut√© sliced garlic and red pepper flakes in olive oil until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and simmer until the sauce begins to thicken.
  5. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook until al dente.
  6. Tear fresh basil leaves into the sauce.
  7. Transfer the pasta directly into the sauce, adding a splash of pasta water.
  8. Toss over medium heat until the sauce coats the pasta beautifully.
  9. Plate with a drizzle of olive oil and fresh cheese.
