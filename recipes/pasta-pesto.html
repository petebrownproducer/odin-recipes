Pasta Pesto

Description

Yummy yummy pasta with pesto

Ingrediemts

  • Spaghetti
  • Pesto

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add salt to the water
  3. Add the spaghetti to the water
  4. Cook until aldente
  5. Put the pesto in a seperate large bowl
  6. Add the spaghetti into the bowl, keep the pasta water in the pan
  7. Add a little pasta water into the bowl and toss to emulsify to the correct sauciness
  8. Plate and add cheese and basil
